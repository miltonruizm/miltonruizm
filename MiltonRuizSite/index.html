Research Scientist in the Department of Physics at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign studying compact binary mergers in magne- tized environments, including binary black holes in gaseous disks, black hole–neutron star, neutron star binaries, and exotic objects such as ergostars. The goal of my research is to predict and correlate observable gravitational-waves and electromagnetic signatures from these events. Also, strongly interested in alternative theories of gravity and mathematical aspects of numerical relativity. I have been supported by grants from the National Science Foundation (NSF) and the National Aeronautics and Space Agency (NASA).
