Team

Over the last ten years, I have been tackling long-standing problems in theoretical astrophysics and numerical relativity. Much of my work centers around simulating the inspiral and merger of binary compact objects in full general relativity. I have helped to develop the Illinois GRMHD code, one of the most advanced codes in the world capable of simultaneously integrating the Einstein equations coupled to the equations of ideal general relativity magnetohydrodynamics and GR force-free electrodynamics.
